Who is that blob ?

The blob, also known as Physarum polycephalum is an organism belonging to the protists class of the genus Physaraceae. It provoked discussion for its ability to learn, and specifically its ability to transmit its knowledge to others.

For few years from now, the scientific community is buzzing and still find new interesting properties to this little organism.
